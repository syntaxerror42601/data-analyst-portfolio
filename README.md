# Retail & Marketplace Operations Analysis

Analysis of 100,000+ e-commerce transactions to identify operational 
trends, fulfillment performance gaps, and seller risk signals.

## Dataset
Olist E-commerce Dataset — Kaggle | 99,441 orders across 6 relational 
tables | 2016–2018

## Tools
MySQL · DBeaver · Excel

## Key Findings
- Strong correlation between delivery time and customer satisfaction revealed supply chain inefficiencies across remote states
- High value product sellers consistently received higher customer satisfaction scores than high volume low margin sellers
- Consistent platform growth from 2017 to 2018 with decelerating growth rate indicating a maturing marketplace
